WAYFARER VIDEO GAME
School Project
Wayfarer is an open world, role-player video game aimed at language learning. In this game, players travel the world, becoming immersed in the country and culture that they choose to visit. While doing so, they also learn the language of that country or region through tasks, exploration, note taking, and interacting with native speakers.
