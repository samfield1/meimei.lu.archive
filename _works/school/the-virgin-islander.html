THE VIRGIN ISLANDER MAGAZINE
School Project
The Virgin Islander is a fictional lifestyle magazine about life and love on the U.S. Virgin Islands, and this particular issue focuses on characters from the novel “Land of Love and Drowning” by Tiphanie Yanique. The character names and storylines are entirely from the book, and this magazine brings that story to life.
