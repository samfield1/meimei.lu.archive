CARMEN, CARMEN MAGAZINE
School Project
Carmen, Carmen is a fanzine which explores the world of singer and songwriter Lana del Rey. It examines the possibilities of other mediums where Lana del Rey could also showcase her artistry, such as if she were also an author or an actress. The zine contains tailored collages for each possibility, which are visual representations of her atmospheric, flowery music.
