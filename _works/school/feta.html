FETA CAFE REBRAND
School Project
Feta is a local DC coffee shop that also serves Mediterranean picks, Bulgarian delights, and Middle Eastern samplings. In this project of rebranding Feta, the challenge was to change the branding of the cafe to fully embrace its Eastern European roots. By doing so, it would stand out amongst other coffee shops by proudly displaying its cultural roots and unique personality.
