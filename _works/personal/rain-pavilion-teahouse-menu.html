RAIN PAVILLION
TEAHOUSE MENU
Personal Project
A common typographic project I always wanted to try was to design a menu, with an added challenge of including both Chinese and English on it. For this project, I took the menu and name of an existing teahouse in Beijing, China, and redesigned it. This teahouse is one of my favorite places in China, and I wanted to create a menu that reflects its gentle and peaceful atmosphere.
